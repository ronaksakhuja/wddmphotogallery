About Us

PhotoStash provides the main functionality of uploading the photos with file name and displaying them in the gallery. Click on + Sign to upload a photo, which is at the bottom of the home page. You can magnify the photos upon clicking on it and close it with a close sign. If you want to delete the picture, please click on the delete button on the photo. If Clicked on Logout, then the page will redirect to login pagin.
